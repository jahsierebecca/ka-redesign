About Us

Randy Karnes, Principal & General Partner

Randy’s experience spans thirty plus years in the areas of accounting, auditing, finance, and business management at Fortune 1000 companies such as Qwest Communications, and the international engineering firm, Morrison Knudsen. In addition to the owning and managing the KARNES ALLEN GROUP, Executive Search, Randy owned Snelling Professional Services for seven years in the executive recruiting and staffing industry, as well as. the ownership and management of a Healthcare business for ten years.

The KARNES ALLEN GROUP benefits greatly from Randy’s ability to draw on his business acumen and recruiting expertise. His specialties include – leadership positions in finance, accounting, auditing, operations management, and nursing within the healthcare industry. He holds a BS in Business and Accounting from Boise State University.
